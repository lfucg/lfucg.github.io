Residential Property Value

Average tax value of single-family dwellings, condominiums and townhomes for Voting Precinct.

Why is this important?

Average residential property value is a good indicator of the health of individual properties themselves in a given area. High property value in an area indicates well maintained properties in that area and is a good indicator of the desirability of an area.

About the Data

The fair cash value for all residential properties in a voting precinct divided by the total number of residential properties in that precinct.

Source: Fayette County Property Valuation Administrator, 2011-2014
